Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I track my order?

    You can track your order by visiting the Orders page. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order is shipped.

  • What is your return policy?

    We offer a 30-day return policy on all products. Items must be in original condition with tags attached.

  • How can I cancel my order?

    You can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it. Visit the Orders page and click on "Cancel Order".

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash on delivery.
